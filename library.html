About the Library

With the establishment of the Institute, the library started its journey as one of the inception, it has been playing a pivotal role in teaching and learning programmes of the Institution. The library is in the ground floor, block-C.
The aim of the Library is to serve the needs of our faculty,researchers,students and the staff. It acts as a center for the collection of literature predominantly related to science, technology, management and its allied subjects, bound period.


Library

Silence is to be maintained. No discussion permitted inside the library. Enter your name and Sign in the register to access and issue the books. No personal belongings allowed inside the library. The librarian may recall any book from any user at any time and the user will have to return the same immediately. Using Mobile phones and speakers or headphones is strictly prohibited in the library premises (Except study related after getting permission from the Librarian). Ask library staff for any kind of help. After reading, the books should be kept on the reading tables. Students can issue two books at a time for seven days and can renew for next seven days Issued books should be returned on or before the due date, if it is late, overdue fine will be charged for the delayed period.
